Instagram

Just a quick note on where I stand on the topic of Instagram.

Not sure how it came to this, but I disagree that Instagram is destroying photography. I hate the idea that photography should be this elite affair. And amateur photographers should be ridiculed and called hipsters just because they bought a big camera recently and want to have fun taking beautiful pictures.

Well, beauty of course is seen differently by different people and that's fine. It makes photography (and especially amateur photography) even more interesting.

I want to post more. And I want to use my iPhone to take pictures of sunsets and pasta and cats... Thing is, it's just not my nature.

But everyone else, keep it up, please. üëç
